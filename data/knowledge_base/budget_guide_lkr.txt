Budget guide for laptop buying (approx LKR ranges)
Under LKR 200,000: basic study laptops, prefer 8GB+ and SSD; avoid heavy creative work
LKR 200,000 - 300,000: solid student machines; aim for 16GB RAM if possible
LKR 300,000 - 450,000: better CPU/GPU options; entry gaming or stronger productivity
LKR 450,000 - 650,000: strong gaming/creator options and premium ultrabooks
Above LKR 650,000: premium MacBooks / high-end gaming and creator laptops
Rule: prioritize RAM and SSD over tiny differences in CPU marketing names
Rule: leave 10-15% budget buffer for bag, mouse, or warranty extensions
Note: market prices change; treat ranges as approximate guidance for recommendations
