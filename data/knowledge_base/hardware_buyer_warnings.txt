Hardware buyer warnings for laptops
Soldered RAM: cannot upgrade later; buy the RAM size you need now
Soldered storage: some ultraportables cannot add a second SSD easily
Battery marketing vs real use: advertised hours are often best-case
Gaming laptops: strong performance but heavier, louder, weaker battery
8GB RAM reality: enough for light Office use, weak for Chrome + Zoom + IDE together
Price traps: low sticker price with 8GB RAM + 256GB SSD may need expensive upgrades later
Check ports: HDMI, USB-C, and SD card matter for students and creators
Thermal note: slim metal laptops can throttle under long heavy loads
